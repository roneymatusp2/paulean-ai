St. Paul's graduates enroll in three or four-year courses at colleges or universities. High academic standards are maintained throughout all academic years. Below is a list of our pupils' destinations over the past five years:

 

### UNITED KINGDOM

University of Cambridge, Durham University, University of the Arts, London, University of Leeds, University College London, London School of Economics, University of Warwick, City University of London, University of Sussex, King's College, Northeastern University of Birmingham, Queen's University, Belfast.

### THE UNITED STATES and CANADA
