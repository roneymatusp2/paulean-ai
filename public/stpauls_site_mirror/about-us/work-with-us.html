    • Why St. Paul's

Our talented team are passionate about education. Together we strive, every day, to deliver our ambition: to do the best we can, in everything we do and to encourage our pupils to become caring learners who seek to have the best possible impact in the world.

We are a diverse, exciting and vibrant learning community, located in the heart of São Paulo’s green and pleasant Jardins district.

We provide students with excellent preparation for IGCSE and IB exams, but more even important, we are here to prepare them be better contributors to a better world.

Why work at St. Paul's

At St. Paul's pupils are at the heart of everything we do. Every one of our staff is dedicated to every child’s personal development, growth, and success.

We seek candidates who are highly qualified, experienced, and who are keen to make a tangible difference in the world. Above all, we’re looking for people share the values which we strive to embody every day, throughout our school.
