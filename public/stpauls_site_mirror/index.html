Welcome to St. Paul's

Introduction

We are the British school of São Paulo. At St. Paul’s we always strive to be the best we can be. We have the courage of our convictions, essential values, and the freedom to imagine and create. This is achieved through our high-quality British and Brazilian holistic education which drives the personal and academic development of pupils, within a caring, inclusive and united community.  

Read more about us

Early Years

In the Pre-Prep School, pupils create strong and lasting foundations for later life.  

Prep School

Prep School teaching and learning combines the Brazilian curriculum with the National Curriculum of England.

Senior School

We provide a broad and balanced curriculum in the Senior School; we teach skills, knowledge, concepts and attitudes in all curriculum areas.

University & Careers

Pupils in the Senior School, and especially in the Sixth Form, take part in a full programme of activities to prepare them for applications to universities and for life beyond St. Paul’s.
