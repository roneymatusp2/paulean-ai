GLOBAL PRIVACY AND DATA PROTECTION POLICY - ST. PAUL’ S (“ POLICY”)

Update: 15 August 2024

INTRODUCTION

The purpose of this policy is to express the commitment of with your privacy and protection of your data, besides setting rules on processing your personal data, within the scope of the services and features of the websites: www.stpauls.br, www.port.fundacaostpauls.org.br, www.fundacaostpauls.org.br, and the St. Paul’s premises, which together comprise one of our physical units (“our environments”), according to current laws, in a clear and transparent way with you and the market in general.

A condition of your access to and use of our environments’ exclusive features, is that you declare you have read this policy fully and carefully, and that you are fully aware of its contents.

We constantly strive to offer the most effective possible services and features, and to do this we upgrade our systems. This policy may therefore require some adjustment from time to time, and ask you to check for changes whenever you can.

SCOPE

This policy applies to all:

  • PUPILS
  • APPLICANTS FOR SCHOLARSHIPS
  • LEGAL AND FINANCIAL REPRESENTATIVES
  • OLD PAULEANS
  • OUTSOURCED STAFF
  • VISITORS
  • PROSPECTIVE PUPILS AND PARENTS
  • JOB APPLICANTS
  • DONORS

If you wish to examine the data we collect and why we collect them, please click on the option above which applies to you to go directly to the relevant section. It is still important that you read the entire policy to understand the other provisions regarding processing of your data.
